How is the Prediction Calculated?

The finish time prediction is calculated using Riegel's formula.
The formula was published in 1977 by an American research engineer
named Peter Riegel. The formula can be seen below.

T2 = T1(D2/D1)^1.06

Where:

T1 = Completed Race Time
D1 = Completed Race Distance
T2 = Prediction Race Time
D2 = Prediction Race Distance
